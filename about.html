About Me

A little about me...

I am originally from Wisconsin and moved to North Carolina thanks to the Army. This is where I met my wonderful husband, who is a native of North Carolina. We decided to continue living in NC and I don't miss those Wisconsin winters.

I went to school at Wake Technical Community College and have a degree in Computer Programming and Development. Currently, I work for the Raleigh Police Department as a Technology Analyst.

Photography is way for me to be creative and share my skills with others. In my free time, I like to draw, paint, take photos and play video games. I also have two adorable cats which love to cuddle.
